About Dudhsagar Decorators & Sound

A family-run Goan decoration and sound service built on tradition, experience, and trust.

Our Story

Dudhsagar Decorators & Sound began its journey over 20 years ago, rooted deeply in Goan traditions and family values.

The business was first started by the owner’s father, who laid the foundation with dedication, sincerity, and a strong commitment to quality service. What started as a small local setup gradually grew through word of mouth and customer trust.

Today, the responsibility is proudly carried forward by Girish Gawas, who continues the legacy while expanding services to meet modern event needs. Under his leadership, the team has successfully completed 200+ decoration setups and sound rentals across Goa.

From traditional maatov ceremonies to family functions and community events, every setup is handled with care, respect, and attention to detail.
